Who are we and why you need to know

We act as a platform where creatives, the talented or all rounded can provide lecture style masterclass via small hands on communities or via Skype for those who wish to learn around the world from each country. Our teachers vary from students to full time workers within their discipline, taking their personal time out to provide a stream of education for those who may not have this opportunity due to culture differences or financial stability. We endeavour to provide a service for anyone who is seeking to up-skill or learn within a disciplinary yet contemporary manner. With world class masterclasses such as English to Humanities, Design to Fine Arts, Mathematics to IT, we believe everyone is entitled to be an educator or a student.

Why are we necessary in a growing diverse culture?

Australia has such a rich provision of diversity where various races, cultures, educational backgrounds and experiences come together. Therefore we believe as an organisation to use this advantage of diversity to educate and empower individuals in the midst of it all.

We believe that for any society or community to grow we must share all our strengths and combine them into a system which makes educating each other enjoyable in the most efficient and practical way. If you have a with a strong passion and desire to share your knowledge, or you are eager in learning on a casual/full-time basis, we would love to partner with you or whether. We would love to have you.
